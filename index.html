About

Dedicated professional with a rich background.

Dedicated professional with a rich background in the consultancy industry dating back to 2014. From my early days as an Office Assistant at Overseas Educational Center to my recent roles as a Business Development Manager at Sambriddhi Groups and now a Part-Time IELTS Instructor and Full-Time Career Counselor at Professional Educational Consultancy, my journey has been marked by a commitment to educational excellence. I achieved an impressive IELTS score of 8 in 2020, and since then, I've been leveraging my expertise to guide students in achieving language proficiency. My experience also includes a stint as a Part-Time Instructor at Global Education and HR, contributing to my comprehensive understanding of the field. Thrilled to be part of International Career Counseling Center, where I continue to empower individuals to navigate global opportunities with confidence. Let's connect and explore the pathways to your academic and career success!
